Did you know most states offer a tax credit towards new vehicle when you trade in your existing vehicle?
IT'S PRETTY SIMPLE
For example, if you are buying a new car for $32,860 and AutoNation will give you $10,000 for your trade, you will only pay sales tax on the $22,860 difference. If sales tax in your state is 7 percent, the tax would be $1,600. Without the trade value sales tax on the purchase increases to $2,300.
